Excercise

Exercise is a vital part of healthy lifestyle, helping to improve physical fitness, boost moos, and reduce stress. Regular physical activity, whether it’s walking, running, and dancing, or strength training, keeps your body strong, improves hearth health, and increases energy levels.

Exercise also helps with mental clarity and focus, making it easier to tackle daily tasks. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week to reap the full benefits. Staying active doesn’t have to be complicated-find activities you enjoy, and make them part of your daily routine
